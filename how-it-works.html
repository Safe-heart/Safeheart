How It Works

Check a Photo

Upload a photo. We simulate a scan that checks if the photo has been used elsewhere online.

Scan a Chat

Paste in a message. We check for signs of manipulation or scams like requests for money, emotional pressure, or unusual links.

Check a Phone Number

Enter a phone number to simulate a scam check. It may return safe, caution, or alert results.
